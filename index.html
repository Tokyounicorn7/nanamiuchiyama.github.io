Nanami A. Uchiyama

Born in Japan in 1999. She was born with a severe internal strabismusesotropia (internally crooked eyes) that later merged into her theme of uncertainty of subjective memory. Photography is a way for her to relativise the memory and images around her. Next to being a photographer, she is an activist and ally of LGBTQ+ and stand up for children’s rights. She frequently joins and holds philosophy cafes in middle/high schools. Currently a university student of Art History and Philosophy(BA) at Sophia University.
